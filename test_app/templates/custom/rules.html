Start
The player going first starts with four cards in hand, and the player going second starts with five. Both players start on 50 Health.
Each turn consists of an action phase, a learning phase, and an end phase.

1. Action Phase
During your action phase, you may play normal attack cards, special attack cards, and focus cards.
During your opponent's action phase, you may play block cards.
Each of these cards has a Combo cost on the left hand side. Each player starts each action phase with 2 Combo Points. You must pay the Combo cost for any card in order to play it.

Focus cards just do what they say immediately.

Whenever you attack your opponent, they can play a block card from their hand to block the attack. If your opponent doesn't block, the attack hits and deals damage equal to its damage value to them.

Whenever you hit your opponent with a normal attack, you gain 1 Combo Point and they lose 1 Combo Point. Whenever you hit your opponent with a special attack, they lose all their Combo Points.

After you're done attacking and playing focus cards, discard all the cards you played this phase, and move to the learning phase.
